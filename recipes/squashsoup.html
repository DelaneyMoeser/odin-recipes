Squash Soup

Description

A savory warm autumn dish that will knock your soup cravings out of the park. A very thick and creamy dish that comes from the Americas!

Ingredients

  • 2tbsp Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 Large Yellow Onion, Chopped
  • 1 Butternut Squash, peeled, seeded, and cubbed
  • 3 tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Chopped Sage
  • 1/2 tbsp grated ginger
  • 4 cups Vegetable Broth
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot at medium head
  2. Drop in the onion, salt, pepper and saut√© until onions are translucent.
  3. Add squash and cook until soften, stirring occansionally.
  4. Add Garlic, sage, rosemary, and ginger. Stir and cook for 1 minute, then add in vegetable broth.
  5. Cover and reduce heat to a simmer and cook until squash is fork tender, 20-30min
  6. Use an emersion blend to smooth soup make sure to blend well.
  7. Serve with crisp bread and Enjoy!
