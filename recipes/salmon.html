Salmon

Description

Salmon is a high protein dish that has a light fish taste. Can be enjoyed many different ways, and taste great oven-baked.

Many enjoy salmon paired with vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 Salmon Cuts
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
  • 2 tsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 tsp Italian Herb Seasoning
  • 1 Lemon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degree and grease large pan and place salmon and vegetables of choice on pan
  2. Stir together olive oil, garlic, herbs, and juice of 1/2 lemon. Spoon over the salmon cuts and ensure full coverage.
  3. Thinly slice remainder of lemon and top salmon
  4. Bake salmon and vegetables for about 35min until cooked through and salmon falls apart with the touch of a fork.
  5. Garnish salmon with fresh thyme or parsley and Enjoy!
